ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE - FALL 2021

 

This course is an introductory survey of Artificial Intelligence (AI). The course will cover the history, theory, and computational methods of AI. Basic concepts include representation of knowledge and computational methods for reasoning.

 

The class meets every Monday and Wednesday, 6PM - 7:15PM, Braker Hall 001.
